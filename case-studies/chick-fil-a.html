Supply App

Chick-fil-A · Internal Logistics

Client

Chick-fil-A

Role

Lead Product Designer

Platform

iOS Mobile App

Agency

Clade Design

Restaurant operators needed a faster, more reliable way to manage supply orders on the floor — without leaving their shift or hunting through legacy systems. We built a net-new internal logistics app from the ground up.

The Problem

Chick-fil-A operators were managing supply orders through a patchwork of spreadsheets and outdated desktop tools — systems not built for the pace of a restaurant floor. We set out to design a purpose-built mobile app that could handle ordering, tracking, and inventory management in a single, intuitive experience.

Research & Process

We conducted on-site field research at multiple Chick-fil-A locations, shadowing operators during peak hours to understand how they moved through the kitchen, what information they needed at a glance, and where the current tools were creating friction. This informed a clear set of design principles: speed, clarity, and minimal cognitive load.

Outcome

The app shipped to a pilot group of operators and received strong internal adoption. Key workflows that previously took several minutes were reduced to under 30 seconds. The project established a design system foundation that Chick-fil-A's internal team continued to build on.
