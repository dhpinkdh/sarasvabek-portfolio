About Me

I am a multidisciplinary Product Designer with over a decade of core industry experience. From early-stage startups to scaling major enterprise tools, I have specialized in creating robust B2B platforms and sleek, high-conversion B2C applications.

Currently, I serve as a Staff Growth Product Designer at Adobe, where I focus on strategic experiments and optimizing core generative AI flows that impact millions of creators globally. Prior to this, I founded and scaled my own design agency, Clade Design, partnering with teams to refine their branding and digital strategy from the ground up.

When I'm not pushing pixels, you can find me diving into 3D modeling, exploring game engines like Unity, or giving back to the design community through speaking engagements and mentorship.
